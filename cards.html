Chronicles of the Deep Ocean

Explore strange new depths

Bioluminescent organisms illuminate the abyssal zone at depths exceeding four thousand meters. Hydrothermal vents along the mid-ocean ridge support entire ecosystems independent of sunlight. The pressure at hadal depths would crush conventional submarines. Sonar mapping has revealed underwater mountain ranges taller than anything found on land. Marine snow drifts down continuously from the sunlit zone above.
