Chronicles of the Deep Ocean

Explore strange new depths

Bioluminescent organisms illuminate the abyssal zone at depths exceeding four thousand meters. Hydrothermal vents along the mid-ocean ridge support entire ecosystems independent of sunlight. The pressure at hadal depths would crush conventional submarines. Sonar mapping has revealed underwater mountain ranges taller than anything found on land.

Thermohaline circulation drives the global conveyor belt connecting all major ocean basins. Upwelling zones along continental shelves bring nutrient-rich water to the surface, fueling massive plankton blooms visible from orbit. Deep sea trenches descend to nearly eleven kilometers below sea level.
